# LinkedIn Quick-Start Checklist

## Before You Post Anything

### Profile (Day 1–2)
- [ ] Rewrite headline — use ICP's language, not your job title
- [ ] Add company descriptor: `Company → What it does`
- [ ] Set functional title (not "Founder" or "Consultant")
- [ ] Match industry to your posting topics
- [ ] Banner communicates value visually
- [ ] About section: Problem → Solution → Proof → CTA (no autobiography)
- [ ] Featured section: newsletter sign-up or booking link first
- [ ] Custom CTA button activated and linked
- [ ] Services section filled with ICP keywords
- [ ] Pass the stranger test (10-second clarity check)

### Research (Day 2–4)
- [ ] Identify 20–30 creators in your niche (5K–30K followers, ICP engagement)
- [ ] Study their top 5 posts — what made each one work?
- [ ] Identify 2–3 content formats that consistently perform in your niche
- [ ] Build engagement list (activity feed URLs, not profile URLs)
- [ ] Identify what's *missing* in your niche — what nobody's saying

### Infrastructure (Day 3–5)
- [ ] Notion kanban set up (Ideas / In Progress / Ready / Posted)
- [ ] Content bank sources identified (sales calls, SOPs, frameworks)
- [ ] AI writing tool configured with: your role, ICP, voice, writing rules, example posts, posts you hate
- [ ] Posting schedule locked into calendar (3–5x per week)

---

## Weekly Execution

### Daily (30 min)
- [ ] 30 min strategic commenting before opening drafts
- [ ] Reply to every comment on your own posts (within 2 hours when possible)

### Each Post
- [ ] Choose intent: Growth / Authority / Conversion / Personal
- [ ] Write body first, mine for hook second
- [ ] First 50 words signal topic + audience clearly
- [ ] Include a visual (image, carousel, one-pager, screenshot)
- [ ] CTA matches intent
- [ ] Ask: "Would my ICP save this?" If no — add something tactical

### Weekly
- [ ] Check content mix: Growth 35–40%, Authority 30–35%, Conversion 20%, Personal 10%
- [ ] Add new ideas to Notion bank (capture everything)
- [ ] Review what performed — double down on winners

---

## Monthly
- [ ] Audit profile — does it still reflect what you post about?
- [ ] Review 20 posts — is there a clear topical pattern? (content fingerprint)
- [ ] Check if any How To content can be reframed as How I
- [ ] Review engagement list — add/remove creators

---

## Red Flags (Stop Immediately)
- Using engagement pods
- Commenting "Great post!" or generic AI responses
- Hashtag stuffing (5+ hashtags)
- Posting without a clear topic lane
- Going more than 3 days without replying to your own post comments
- Writing about 5+ different topics per month (no fingerprint)
