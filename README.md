# Content Engine

## What It Is

Content Engine is an AI-powered platform that helps creators, marketing teams, and agencies generate, optimize, and publish high-quality content at scale. It combines intelligent content creation with SEO optimization, image generation, analytics tracking, and team collaboration—handling everything from research to distribution in one unified workflow.

## How It Works

**1. Research & Brief**
Input your topic, keyword, or existing content. The platform researches search intent, analyzes competitors, and generates a structured brief with recommended headings, NLP terms, word count targets, and internal linking suggestions.

**2. Generate**
AI creates an SEO-optimized draft tailored to your brand voice, tone, and style. Optional: upload your own article, blog post, PDF, YouTube video, or podcast URL for repurposing.

**3. Edit & Refine**
Inline editing tools let you rewrite sections, adjust tone, expand or shorten paragraphs, and fix SEO without leaving the platform.

**4. Review & Approve**
If you're on a team, submit for review. Editors leave comments, suggest changes, and approve before publishing. Approval workflow gates publication.

**5. Enhance**
Auto-generate featured images and platform-specific social cards. System checks for plagiarism and AI detection. Audit SEO score and brand voice alignment.

**6. Schedule & Publish**
Queue content to your blog, email, social channels, or RSS with automatic scheduling. Track performance with real-time analytics.
